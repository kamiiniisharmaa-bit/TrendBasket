About TrendBasket

Est. 2020  ·  New York, NY

Our Mission

To curate the most exciting trends and deliver them to your doorstep with care, speed, and style — making fashion accessible for everyone.

Our Vision

To become the world's favorite destination for trend-forward, sustainable fashion that empowers individuals to express themselves confidently.

Who We Are

TrendBasket was born from a simple idea: shopping should be exciting, easy, and inspiring. We're a team of fashion enthusiasts, tech lovers, and customer-obsessed dreamers who believe that style is for everyone.

From sneakers to accessories, we handpick each item to ensure quality, originality, and value. Our collections are refreshed weekly to keep you ahead of the curve.

What We Stand For

  • Authenticity: Every product is sourced from trusted brands and verified suppliers.
  • Sustainability: We're committed to reducing waste and offering eco-friendly options.
  • Community: We listen, we learn, and we grow with our customers.
  • Innovation: From AI-powered recommendations to seamless checkout, we're always evolving.

Meet the Team

Behind TrendBasket is a diverse crew of buyers, stylists, developers, and support specialists who work tirelessly to bring you the best experience. We're based in New York, but our reach is global.

  • Elena V. – Co-Founder & Creative Director
  • Marcus J. – Co-Founder & Head of Operations

Our Story

What started as a small pop-up shop in Brooklyn quickly grew into a digital destination. In 2020, we launched TrendBasket with a mission to disrupt the fast-fashion industry by offering curated, high-quality pieces at fair prices. Today, we serve thousands of customers across the US and Europe — and we're just getting started.

Giving Back

We believe in fashion with a purpose. A portion of every sale goes to organizations that support education, environmental conservation, and social equity. We also partner with local artisans to preserve traditional craftsmanship.
