Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026  ·  TrendBasket

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as when you create an account, place an order, or contact us. This may include your name, email, shipping address, and payment details.

  • Account data: username, password (encrypted), preferences.
  • Transaction data: order history, payment method.
  • Usage data: pages viewed, time spent, referring URLs.

2. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

  • Essential cookies: required for core functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand how you use the site.
  • Marketing cookies: used for targeted ads (with your consent).

3. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to process orders, provide customer support, improve our services, and send you promotional offers (if you opt in).

  • Order fulfillment and delivery updates.
  • Personalize recommendations and product suggestions.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure site security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted partners who help us operate the site (e.g., payment processors, shipping carriers) under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption (SSL/TLS), access controls, and regular monitoring. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You can also withdraw consent for marketing communications.

  • Access: request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: update inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: request permanent removal of your account.

7. Children's Privacy

TrendBasket does not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The current version will always be available on this page. Significant changes will be notified via email or site notice.
